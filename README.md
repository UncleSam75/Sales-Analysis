## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive **Sales Analytics Dashboard** developed using **Microsoft Power BI** with the AdventureWorks dataset. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance by analyzing sales, customers, products, returns, profitability, and geographical distribution.

The objective of this project is to help business stakeholders monitor KPIs, identify sales trends, evaluate customer behavior, analyze product performance, and make data-driven business decisions.

---

# 📊 Dashboard Pages

## 1️⃣ Executive Dashboard

Provides a high-level overview of overall business performance.

### Key KPIs

* 💰 Total Revenue
* 📈 Total Profit
* 📦 Total Orders
* 🔄 Return Rate

### Visualizations

* Revenue Trend Analysis
* Orders by Category
* Monthly Revenue
* Monthly Orders
* Monthly Returns
* Product Performance Table
* Most Ordered Product Type
* Most Returned Product Type

---

## 2️⃣ Customer Detail Dashboard

Analyzes customer purchasing behavior and revenue generation.

### Key Metrics

* Total Customers
* Revenue Per Customer
* Customer Growth Trend
* Top Customers by Revenue
* Orders by Occupation
* Orders by Income Level

### Insights

* Highest revenue-generating customers
* Customer segmentation
* Revenue contribution
* Customer ordering patterns

---

## 3️⃣ Product Detail Dashboard

Provides product-level sales analysis.

### Features

* Product Selection
* Dynamic Price Adjustment Simulation
* Monthly Revenue vs Target
* Monthly Orders vs Target
* Monthly Profit vs Target
* Performance Trends

### Interactive Elements

* What-if Parameter
* Product Metric Selector
* Dynamic KPI Cards

---

## 4️⃣ Geographic Sales Analysis

Visualizes global sales distribution.

### Includes

* Sales by Country
* Sales by Region
* Interactive Map
* Continent Filter

Helps identify high-performing geographic markets.

---

## 5️⃣ Decomposition Tree Analysis

Power BI AI visual used to investigate Return Rate.

Users can drill down by:

* Continent
* Country
* Category
* Subcategory
* Product

This enables quick root-cause analysis for business performance.
